Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Overview

At MPH Booking System, we strive to provide excellent service and a fair refund policy. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions under which refunds are issued for court bookings. Please read this policy carefully before making a booking.

By completing a booking on our platform, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to this Refund Policy.

2. Cancellation and Refund Schedule

Refund eligibility is determined by when you cancel your booking relative to the scheduled booking time. Our cancellation policy is as follows:

Cancellation Timeframe Refund Amount
24 hours or more before booking time 100% Full Refund
12-24 hours before booking time 50% Partial Refund
Less than 12 hours before booking time No Refund
After booking time has passed No Refund
No-show (did not cancel or attend) No Refund

üí° Tip: To receive a full refund, cancel your booking at least 24 hours before your scheduled time. You can cancel anytime through the "My Bookings" page.

3. How to Cancel a Booking

Cancelling your booking is simple and can be done in a few steps:

1Log in to your account on the MPH Booking System

2Navigate to "My Bookings" from the main menu or profile dropdown

3Find the booking you wish to cancel in your list of active bookings

4Click the "Cancel Booking" button next to the booking

5Confirm the cancellation when prompted

6Receive confirmation - You will receive a cancellation confirmation email with refund details

4. Refund Processing

4.1 Processing Time

Once your cancellation is confirmed:

  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  • The refund will be credited to the original payment method used for the booking
  • You will receive an email notification when the refund has been processed
  • Depending on your bank or payment provider, it may take an additional 3-5 business days for the funds to appear in your account

4.2 Refund Method

Refunds are issued to the original payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Card: Refunded to the card used for payment
  • Digital Wallets: Refunded to the wallet account
  • Bank Transfer: For special cases, refunds may be processed via bank transfer (requires verification)

5. Non-Refundable Circumstances

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Refunds will NOT be issued in the following situations:

  • Late Cancellations: Bookings cancelled less than 12 hours before the scheduled time
  • No-Shows: Failure to attend your booking without prior cancellation
  • Violation of Terms: Bookings cancelled due to violation of our Terms of Service or facility rules
  • Facility Misuse: Damage to facility property or inappropriate conduct
  • Fraudulent Activity: Any fraudulent bookings or payment activities
  • Past Bookings: Requests for refunds after the booking time has passed

6. Facility Closures and Emergency Situations

6.1 Facility-Initiated Cancellations

If we need to cancel your booking due to:

  • Facility maintenance or repairs
  • Emergency closures
  • Weather-related issues
  • Technical problems with the facilities
  • Other circumstances beyond your control

You will receive a 100% full refund, regardless of when the cancellation occurs. The refund will be processed automatically within 3-5 business days.

6.2 Force Majeure

In cases of force majeure events (natural disasters, government restrictions, pandemics, etc.), we will work with affected users on a case-by-case basis to provide fair solutions, which may include:

  • Full refunds
  • Rebooking at a later date without additional charges
  • Credit toward future bookings

7. Modification vs. Cancellation

Please Note: Our system currently does not support booking modifications. If you need to change your booking date, time, or court type, you must:

  1. Cancel your existing booking (subject to our refund policy)
  2. Make a new booking with your desired details

To avoid losing money on late cancellations, please ensure you book the correct details initially, and cancel early if changes are needed.

8. Partial Refunds

For cancellations made 12-24 hours before your booking time, you will receive a 50% refund:

  • The refund amount is calculated as 50% of the total booking cost
  • The remaining 50% is retained as a late cancellation fee
  • This policy helps us manage court availability and compensate for last-minute cancellations

9. Refund Confirmation

After cancelling a booking eligible for a refund, you will receive:

  • Immediate: A cancellation confirmation email with your booking details and refund information
  • Within 5-7 days: A refund processing notification when the refund has been initiated
  • Original payment method: The refund credited to your account

Each confirmation email includes:

  • Your booking confirmation number
  • Booking details (date, time, court, sport)
  • Cancellation date and time
  • Refund amount and expected processing time

10. Disputed Refunds

If you believe you are entitled to a refund that was not provided, or if you have questions about a refund decision:

  • Contact our support team within 14 days of the booking date
  • Provide your booking confirmation number and details
  • Explain the reason for your dispute
  • Include any supporting documentation (screenshots, emails, etc.)

We will review your case and respond within 3-5 business days. Refund disputes are handled on a case-by-case basis, and our decision is final.

11. Special Circumstances

We understand that unexpected situations may arise. If you have special circumstances that prevented you from cancelling within the required timeframe (e.g., medical emergencies, family emergencies), please contact our support team:

  • Provide detailed information about your situation
  • Include relevant documentation (medical certificates, etc.)
  • Submit your request within 7 days of the booking date

While we cannot guarantee approval, we will review each case with consideration and may offer a partial refund or credit for future bookings.

12. Contact for Refund Inquiries

For questions about refunds, cancellations, or to dispute a refund decision, please contact us:

Email: support@mph.com
Subject Line: "Refund Inquiry - [Your Confirmation Number]"
Location: Multi-Purpose Hall, TUS Athlone
Hours: Monday - Sunday, 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM

What to Include:

  • Your full name and student ID
  • Booking confirmation number
  • Booking date and time
  • Reason for refund request
  • Any supporting documentation

13. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to our platform. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the modified policy.

However, any changes to this policy will not affect bookings made prior to the effective date of the change.
